Eco Horizon Initiative (E-HI)

With 18 years of passionate volunteering experience in sustainability, we are a team of eco-enthusiasts dedicated to empowering a greener future. Through learning journeys, workshops, team-building activities, and event management, Eco Horizon Initiative(E-HI) inspire meaningful action toward sustainability.

Mission

To share and educate members of the public towards stewardship of a safe clean sustainable environment.
